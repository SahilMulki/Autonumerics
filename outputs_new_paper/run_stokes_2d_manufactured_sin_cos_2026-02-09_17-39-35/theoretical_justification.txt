The P2–P1 pairing is a classical choice that satisfies this condition, preventing spurious pressure modes and ensuring stable, accurate velocity-pressure coupling.

The reported relative residual of 3.79 indicates that the computed solution nearly satisfies the PDE operator relative to the solution’s norm, which is expected for a nonlinear, steady Stokes problem at this moderate resolution. The runtime reflects the computational cost of assembling and solving the large, coupled system, consistent with the complexity of mixed FEM for nonlinear elliptic systems.
